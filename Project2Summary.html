Software Support, UAT & Incident Resolution

Digital Transformation & System Change Project

Project Overview

As part of a large-scale digital transformation project - Moving from SAP to Oracle based solution. Provided end-to-end software support, user acceptance testing (UAT), and incident resolution for the rollout of a new crm/erp system replacing multiple legacy applications across departments. Ensured stability, user adoption, and seamless transition to new processes while maintaining critical service levels.

Key Responsibilities

  • Managed incidents and issues, root cause analysis, and resolutions of system-related issues.
  • Supported users across multiple business units during UAT, capturing and validating test outcomes.
  • logging all issues and remediation progress through Jira and Self Built trackers.
  • Collaborated with developers, analysts, and stakeholders to reproduce, prioritize, and resolve functional bugs.
  • Delivered training and onboarding materials to guide end-users through new workflows post-implementation.
  • Monitored data integrity and process alignment during post-go-live stabilization.

Technologies & Tools Used

  • Jira for incident and change management
  • SQL Server & Excel for data validation and troubleshooting
  • Power BI & SharePoint for reporting and communication tracking
  • Microsoft Teams for remote UAT coordination and stakeholder updates
  • Confluence for documentation and UAT process tracking

Impact & Results

  • Resolved 95% of critical incidents within SLA during hypercare and transition phases.
  • Reduced UAT cycle time by 60% through improved test coordination and defect management workflows.
  • Enhanced end-user confidence through structured support and proactive communication channels.
  • Improved data accuracy and system reliability through continuous validation and feedback loops.
  • Successfully supported over 700 users during phased system rollout with minimal downtime.
