Collaboration, Documentation & Training

Leadership, Enablement & BI Process Improvement

Project Overview

As a BI and Data Leader, I’ve delivered comprehensive system and reporting tool training to teams across multiple departments. These initiatives focused on enhancing data literacy, process consistency, and tool adoption. Developed and maintained collaborative documentation systems to ensure long-term scalability and knowledge sharing across teams.

Key Responsibilities

  • Provided structured BI tool training (Power BI, Excel, SQL, DAX) for analysts, managers, and executives.
  • Led collaborative sessions to align reporting standards, data governance, and visualization best practices.
  • Built and maintained documentation portals on Confluence and SharePoint for self-service learning.
  • Created onboarding materials, walkthrough videos, and training dashboards for new team members.
  • Mentored junior analysts, improving efficiency and standardization across the BI team.

Technologies & Tools Used

  • Power BI, SQL Server, and DAX
  • Confluence, SharePoint, and Teams for documentation and collaboration
  • Microsoft Stream for training video hosting
  • Excel VBA and Power Query for training materials and automation demos

Impact & Results

  • Increased Power BI adoption and reduced reporting bottlenecks across departments by 75%.
  • Improved data quality and confidence through documented validation processes.
  • Enhanced cross-functional communication between IT, Finance, and Operations teams.
  • Established a reusable library of dashboards, templates, and guides for ongoing training.
